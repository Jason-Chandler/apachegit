Jack and the Beanstalk

There once was a worthless low-life named Jack.
He really had nothing to do most of the time,
so he was always a big fatheaded jerk to whomever he came across.

One day he came across a beanstalk. This beanstalk was an upstanding member of society.
He always helped out the homeless,
he didn't cut people off on the freeway,
he flossed everyday,
sometimes he even calmly wrote to his congressman when he had a complaint!
This beanstalk was grade A.

But Jack didn't care. Jack was scum who crushes hopes and dreams. He promptly started climbing that beanstalk,
destroying the beanstalk's...stalks as he did so.

The climbing continued for hours...or at least it seemed to because Jack never learned to tell time.
But finally, when his reign of terror was finally done, and Jack had reached the top, he turned back to look upon the carnage that
he had betrothed (this is the appropriate verbage) with his climbing.

His eyes fell upon the face of the crumpled, crippled, crambled beanstalk...
and that's when Jack fell to his knees in the kind of abject terror that only happens in short stories.

For he recognized that face.
It was just like his own.
The beanstalk was none other than his twin brother! #JustSpookyEndings
